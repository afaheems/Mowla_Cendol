Discover a World of Refreshing Treats

Escape the heat and tantalize your taste buds with our delectable selection of traditional Malaysian desserts. At Mowla Cendol, we proudly serve a variety of icy delights, each crafted with the finest ingredients and served with a smile.

A Taste of Heritage: Mowla Cendol's Cendol Haven

Mowla Cendol isn't just a name; it's a legacy. Rooted in the heart of Gombak, our humble stall has been serving up bowls of nostalgia, one spoonful at a time. For generations, we've been crafting the perfect cendol, a timeless dessert that has captured the hearts of locals and visitors alike.

Our Story

Our journey began with Mowla, a skilled artisan who perfected the art of making cendol. Passed down through generations, this secret recipe has been cherished and refined, ensuring that every bowl is a testament to tradition. Our family's passion for sharing this delightful dessert has driven us to open our own stall, where we continue to honor our heritage.

Our Signature Desserts:

  • Cendol: A classic Malaysian dessert featuring silky smooth green rice flour jelly, coconut milk, palm sugar syrup, and red bean.
  • Ais Kacang: A colorful and refreshing dessert with shaved ice, red beans, sweet corn, jellies, and a drizzle of condensed milk and syrup.
  • ABC: A hearty dessert with a mix of shaved ice, red beans, sweet corn, jellies, sago pearls, and a generous scoop of ice cream.
